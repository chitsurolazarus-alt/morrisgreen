Who We Are

Morris Green Landscaping is a premier outdoor solutions provider dedicated to transforming ordinary spaces into extraordinary landscapes. We are a family-owned business that has been serving clients across South Africa for over 15 years.

Our journey began in 2010 with a simple vision: to create beautiful, sustainable outdoor spaces that bring joy to our clients. Today, we've grown into a trusted name in the landscaping industry, known for our attention to detail, innovative designs, and commitment to excellence.

What sets us apart is our holistic approach to landscaping. We don't just install features; we create complete outdoor experiences that harmonize with your lifestyle and the natural environment. From the initial consultation to the final touches, we work closely with you to bring your vision to life.
